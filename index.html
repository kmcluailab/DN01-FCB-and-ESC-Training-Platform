Scientech DN01 - FCB and ESC Training Platform

System Overview

Explore hands-on UAV electronics and control systems through a modular platform. Designed for education and experimentation, this system simplifies learning UAV technologies.

  • Flight Controller Board (FCB): Manages flight control and sensor data.
  • Electronic Speed Controllers (ESCs): Regulate motor speed and ensure precise thrust control.
  • Sensors: IMU, GPS, and Barometer for navigation and stability.
  • Power Distribution & RC Receiver: Distributes power and receives pilot commands.

Flight Controller Specifications (Click to Toggle)

  • Microcontroller: STM32F405, ARM Cortex-M4 @ 168 MHz
  • Firmware: ArduPilot, PX4
  • Interfaces: UART, I2C, SPI, PWM, USB
  • Memory: 16 MB Flash
  • Input Voltage: 5V
  • Dimensions: 36mm x 36mm
